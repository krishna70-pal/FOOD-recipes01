Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed lettuce (washed & torn)
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion in a bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk olive oil, lemon juice (or vinegar), salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve immediately.
